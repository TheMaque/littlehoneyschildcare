What does a day look like for my kids?
  1. Programs developed by expert educators—for every age and learning style Our overarching Life Essentials philosophy gives your child the physical, emotional, technological and cognitive foundation they need to thrive in school and in life.
  2. Care and curriculum go hand in hand Our caring teachers create a nurturing atmosphere for your child to learn—safely guiding and encouraging them through every aspect of their day.
  3. Health Essentials = Peace of mind Every Kiddie Academy location follows Health Essentials, our enhanced wellness and disinfection guidelines designed to ensure the health of your child and family, as well as our staff members.
  4. Preparing children to succeed at every level We’re dedicated to developing what is unique in every child. Each of our programs is designed to help your child master the fundamentals and develop a love of learning at their own pace.
